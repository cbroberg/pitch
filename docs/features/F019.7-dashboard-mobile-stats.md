# F019.7 — Dashboard: hide stat cards on mobile + link Total Pitches on desktop

Parent: **F019 — Mobile-first optimization**.

## Motivation

On a phone the three stat cards (Total Pitches / Total Views / Active Links) each take a full-width block, pushing the actually-useful **Recent Pitches** list below the fold — three big numbers with no action. Christian: “De tre cards med stats har INGEN værdi på mobile, så fjern dem på mobile. På desktop skal Total Pitches linke til pitches.”

## Change (`app/(app)/dashboard/page.tsx`)

- Stat grid: `grid gap-4 sm:grid-cols-3` → `hidden gap-4 sm:grid sm:grid-cols-3` — hidden on mobile, unchanged 3-column grid on desktop. Recent Pitches becomes the top content on a phone.
- Total Pitches card wrapped in `<Link href="/pitches">` with `hover:bg-muted/50 hover:border-primary/40 cursor-pointer` + focus-visible ring + `data-testid="dashboard-total-pitches-link"`. Desktop-only (the whole grid is hidden on mobile).

## Verification

Lens iphone-15: no stat cards, Recent Pitches at top. Lens 1280: 3 cards present, Total Pitches clickable. tsc clean.
